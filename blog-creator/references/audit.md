# Outline Audit (Subagent)

Run rankearly MCP's `core-eeat-audit` tool on the outline. Save the result to `./blogs/<topic>/audit.md`.

## Additional Criteria

- **Flow.** Adjacent sections must set up or follow from each other. If two sections feel unrelated side by side — reorder, merge, or cut.
- **Weight matches depth.** Give space to points with real substance. Fold thin points into a parent section or cut them.
- **Structure follows content.** A section with one clear idea needs no h3s. A section covering multiple sub-concepts benefits from h3s.
- **No filler.** Cut trivia, tangents, and footnote-level insights. Fewer strong sections beats comprehensive fluff.
- **Conversion hooks.** Place them where they serve the reader (e.g. product capability as a concrete example, free tier mention in the conclusion). Mark with `[hook: ...]`.
